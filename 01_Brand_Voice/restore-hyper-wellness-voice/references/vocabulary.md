## Therapy-specific approved claims

All benefit claims must lead with "{Therapy} may help to..."

### Cryotherapy

May help to:
- Reduce pain, inflammation and stress
- Promote healing and athletic recovery
- Boost mood, energy and sleep
- Defy signs of aging
- Burn calories and slim down

**Whole Body Cryotherapy:** Sub-zero temperatures of -166°F to -220°F for 2–3 minutes activate the body's natural healing response by constricting blood vessels, driving circulation to the core and triggering physiological pathways for healing.

**Local Cryotherapy:** Targeted cold zeros in on areas that need support — pain, swelling and soreness — to speed recovery, reduce migraine symptoms and reverse signs of aging.

### Red Light Therapy

May help to:
- Improve mood and energy
- Relieve pain and inflammation
- Optimize sleep
- Increase collagen production and skin health
- Improve eye health (clients 40+)

Service: 10–15 minutes, 3–5 times weekly. Also known as Photobiomodulation Therapy. Uses low levels of red and near-infrared light absorbed by mitochondria.

### Infrared Sauna

May help to:
- Promote energy and healing
- Improve circulation and heart health
- Optimize sleep
- Detoxify heavy metals

Service: 15 minutes building to 40 minutes, 3–5 times weekly. Sweat, detox and burn calories with gentle heat.

### Compression

May help to:
- Improve lymphatic function and circulation
- Reduce muscle aches and pains
- Improve flexibility and mobility

Refer to as **Compression**, not "Compression Therapy". Uses NormaTec Pulse Rx 2.0 — the gold standard in Compression. 30–60 minutes per session, 3–5 times weekly. Intended for lymphedema conditions, venous insufficiency, prevention of deep vein thrombosis.

### IV Drip Therapy

May help to:
- Hydrate and cleanse
- Replenish vital nutrients
- Maximize performance and focus

**Tagline:** "Fuel Your Body. Feel the Results."

**Key stat:** 100% bioavailability via IV vs. 15–20% absorption for oral supplements.

Service: 60–90 minutes including TeleMed call and medical intake. Frequency varies by Drip. Member-favorite Drips: Wonder Juice, Unstoppable, Recharge, Hangover, Defender, New Myers, Amplified Beauty.

**Compliance:** Cannot show needles in paid media. Nurses must wear gloves.

### Mild Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (mHbOT)

Always include "Mild" — ours are not medical grade.

May help to: support recovery, oxygenation and cognitive function.

### Skin Health

Hydrafacial™, Neveskin™ Facial / Shape / Tone, Marini® Facials & Peels, HydroPeptide® Facials, DERMAFLASH® Dermaplaning, DERMAPORE+ Extract and Fuse.

Gloves mandatory for Hydrafacial™ (including Keravive); optional for other services.
